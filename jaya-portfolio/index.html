About Me

A Passionate Approach to Nutrition & Wellness

I'm Jaya Yadav — a passionate and motivated Clinical Dietitian with a strong foundation in Nutrition and Dietetics from Vinoba Bhave University, Hazaribagh. I believe in the power of food to heal, support well-being, and make everyday life better.

During my clinical internship at TATA Main Hospital, West Bokaro, I gained invaluable hands-on experience in nutritional assessment, therapeutic diet planning, OPD counseling across specialties like Endocrinology, Nephrology, Cardiology, and Oncology, and daily care of critically ill patients. Now, as a Consultant Dietitian at Fitelo, I channel that experience into personalized, sustainable nutrition plans for every client.

🌱

My Philosophy

I don't believe in fad diets or quick fixes. My approach centers on understanding your unique body, lifestyle, and goals — then crafting a plan that works for your life, not against it.
